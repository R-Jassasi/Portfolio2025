UWE Foundry – Global Game Jam Event

I’ve taken part in Global Game Jam three years in a row, each time with a different setup and experience. In 2023, I joined a brilliant team and led the graphic design efforts—creating game assets, menus, and contributing heavily to the concept and narrative. In 2024, I returned with a more technical role, helping design the game map and build puzzles, while still shaping the concept, scripting, and overall story. In 2025, I took part remotely and completely solo—as an experiment. It was my first time handling everything on my own, from idea to implementation, using Unity to build a small but complete game by myself. Each jam has been a chance to challenge myself in different ways and grow creatively, whether through teamwork or self-led development.
