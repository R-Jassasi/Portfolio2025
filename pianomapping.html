Piano Mapping

3D | Audio-visual installation | Reactive visuals

2025

Piano Mapping III is a collaborative work with composer Max Daviesâ€”an interactive audio-visual installation using a mix of hardware and software including Unity, Resolume, and MainStage. It projects visuals of various scenes and abstract elements that react in real time to the pianist, who plays into a custom-built white piano body connected via a MIDI keyboard. The latest iteration features three movements: a nature scene, an abstract light scene, and a dancing galaxy scene. Each one complements the narration of the three original compositions written for them. In March 2025, we collaborated with the Roath Chamber Orchestra and performed the piece live at Urban Crofters in Cardiff.
